IPL 2025

by Mohammed Siddiq | February 18, 2025

The 18th edition of the Indian Premier League (IPL) is set to commence on March 22, 2025, and conclude with the final on May 25, 2025. This season will feature 10 teams competing across 74 matches in 13 venues throughout India. The group stage will see each team playing 14 matches, with the top four teams advancing to the playoffs, culminating in the final at Eden Gardens in Kolkata.

Opening Match:
  • The season opener will be held at Eden Gardens, where the Kolkata Knight Riders will face the Royal Challengers Bangalore.

Rajat Patidar has been appointed as the new captain, succeeding Faf du Plessis.

Playoff Venues:
  • Eden Gardens will also host Qualifier 2 and the final, marking the first time in a decade that Kolkata will host the IPL final.
